PESTLE Analysis

Our PESTLE-inspired framework enables structured comparison of resilience practices across Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Legal, and Environmental dimensions. Compare Australia, APEC, and global approaches to identify best practices and opportunities for learning.

  • Political – Policy and governance frameworks
  • Economic – Financial mechanisms and recovery funding
  • Social – Community resilience and social cohesion
  • Technological – Innovation and digital infrastructure
  • Legal – Regulatory compliance and liability
  • Environmental – Ecosystem-based approaches

Publishing Workflow

  1. Author drafts a blog post.
  2. Admin reviews the content for accuracy and relevance.
  3. Post is edited if needed.
  4. Post is approved for publication.
  5. Published post appears on the blog listing page.

Moderated Publishing Process

All submitted blog posts are reviewed before publication. Content must be respectful, relevant, accurate, and suitable for public climate engagement.
